Conservation Status & Threats

Many gibbon species are endangered due to habitat loss, hunting, and the illegal pet trade. Protecting their forest homes and ensuring safe corridors is critical for their survival.

Research Activities

Our team conducts acoustic monitoring to study gibbon songs and behavior studies to understand family dynamics and movement patterns. These insights guide our conservation strategies.
