Community Partnerships

Local communities are essential allies in gibbon conservation. Together we develop programs that improve livelihoods while protecting forests.

Our Programs

  • Eco-friendly livelihoods and handicraft cooperatives.
  • Environmental education in village schools.
  • Community guardians monitoring forest edges.

Stories from the Forest

Maya leads a women's cooperative weaving bamboo baskets that provide income while reducing logging pressure.

Local teachers incorporate gibbon ecology into their lessons, inspiring children to become future conservationists.

Community guardians patrol forest trails, recording gibbon calls and deterring poaching.
