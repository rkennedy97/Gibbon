About Our Foundation

The Gibbon Harmony Conservation Foundation was born from a love of gibbons and a commitment to safeguarding tropical forests. Founded by conservationists and community leaders, our mission is to protect gibbons through science-based conservation and to empower the people who share their habitat.

Our Partnerships

We collaborate with universities, wildlife researchers, and local community groups. Together, we carry out field studies, develop sustainable livelihoods, and advocate for policies that keep forests standing.
